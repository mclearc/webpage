Kant on Introspection, Self-Consciousness, and Self-Knowledge

University of Nebraska–Lincoln

May 1-2, 2015

Robert R. Chambers Philosophy Conference

Welcome to the home page for the seventh annual Robert R. Chambers Philosophy Conference on themes related to Kant’s (and Kantian) views on introspection, self-consciousness, and self-knowledge hosted by the Department of Philosophy at UNL on May 1-2, 2015.

Participants

Confirmed speakers include:

  • Andrew Brook (Carleton University)
  • Andrew Chignell (Cornell University)
  • Anil Gomes (Oxford University)
  • Béatrice Longuenesse (New York University)
  • Derk Pereboom (Cornell University)
  • Clinton Tolley (University of California, San Diego)

Accomodations

We will have two blocks of rooms reserved in downtown Lincoln, convenient to the conference venue. More information coming soon!

Travel

Flights daily from both the Lincoln Airport (LNK) and Eppley Airport (OMA) in Omaha, about an hour north of Lincoln.
