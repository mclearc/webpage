Chambers Conference Readings

Perhaps the easiest way to prepare for the conference is to try to get some background knowledge concerning the relevant issues that will be discussed in the speaker’s papers. To that end I include links below to overview articles concerning introspection and self-knowledge, as well as an entry on Kant on self-consciousness (by one of the conference speakers), a draft of a general overview I’ve written on Kant’s view of the mind, and some other relevant articles by a couple of the speakers. A draft of Andrew Brook’s conference paper is also below. If you have further interest or questions about what to read by particular speakers, just let me know.

  • Stanford Encyclopedia Overviews
    • Introspection
    • Self-Knowledge
    • Kant on Self-Consciousness
  • McLear, Internet Encyclopedia Entry on Kant’s Philosophy of Mind (Draft)
  • Brook, “Apperception & Dark Representations” (Paper for the conference)
    • Brook, “Kant: A Unified Representational Base for All Consciousness” (helpful background)
  • Longuenesse, “Kant on ‘I’ and the soul (Paper for the conference)
    • Longuenesse, “Self-Consciousness and Consciousness of One’s Own Body” (helpful background)
    • Longuenesse “Kant on the Identity of Persons” (helpful background)
